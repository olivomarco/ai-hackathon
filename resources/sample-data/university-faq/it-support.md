# IT Support at Northfield University

Northfield University Information Technology provides support for student accounts, campus WiFi, classroom software, printing, device troubleshooting, and approved cloud tools. Most students interact with IT during orientation week to activate multi-factor authentication, connect devices, and install required software.

The primary campus WiFi network is **Northfield Secure**. Students should sign in using their university username and password. A separate **Northfield Guest** network is available for visitors but does not provide access to student-only systems. If a device cannot connect to Northfield Secure, students should forget the network and reconnect after verifying their password and MFA setup.

Currently enrolled students can download software such as Microsoft 365 Apps, Visual Studio Code, and selected engineering tools through the Software Hub portal. Eligible students may also activate **Azure for Students** benefits for learning and experimentation. IT recommends reviewing subscription limits before starting resource-heavy cloud projects.

The IT Helpdesk assists with password resets, account lockouts, printing, laptop support, and software installation questions. Students can submit tickets through the self-service portal or email **help@northfield.edu**. The walk-in help desk is located in Digital Commons, Desk 1, and is open Monday through Friday from **8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.** Extended support chat is available Sunday through Thursday from **7:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.** during the semester.

For urgent classroom technology issues, faculty and students should call **(555) 010-HELP**. Students working on development projects should include screenshots, device details, and error messages when opening a ticket to speed up resolution.
