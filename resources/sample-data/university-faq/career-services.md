# Career Services at Northfield University

Northfield University Career Services helps students prepare for internships, co-ops, full-time roles, graduate school applications, and professional networking. The office works closely with employers in software engineering, cloud computing, cybersecurity, UX, analytics, and product management.

Students can access the **Northfield Careers Portal** to search internships and jobs, register for recruiting events, upload résumés, and book advising appointments. New students are encouraged to complete their career profile and résumé during their first semester so they can take advantage of early internship opportunities.

Career coaches offer help with résumé and cover letter review, interview practice, LinkedIn profiles, networking strategy, salary conversations, and internship search planning. Appointment types include 20-minute drop-ins for quick document review and 45-minute coaching sessions for deeper career planning. Drop-in hours are available Monday through Thursday from **1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.** during the semester.

Northfield hosts two major career fairs each academic year: the **Fall Tech Recruiting Expo** in late September and the **Spring Internship & Innovation Fair** in mid-February. Many employers also hold information sessions on campus or virtually throughout the year. Students seeking internships for the following summer should begin preparing in early fall, especially for competitive engineering and product roles.

Questions can be sent to **careers@northfield.edu**, and employers can contact **recruiting@northfield.edu**. The Career Services office is located in Launch Center, Suite 300, and is open Monday through Friday from **8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.**
